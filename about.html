Who We Are

Two enterprising students, driven by a shared passion for technology and problem-solving, decided to launch their own appliance repair service business. Combining their technical know-how with a desire to help their community, they began offering quick, affordable repairs for common household appliances. To reach more customers and make booking services easier, they built a clean, user-friendly website showcasing their offerings. What started as a small side project quickly grew into a trusted local solution for reliable appliance repair.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair solutions. We pride ourselves on professional service, skilled technicians, and top-notch customer satisfaction.
